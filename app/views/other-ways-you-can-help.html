There are other ways that you can help

We appreciate that you might not feel hosting is the right choice for you now, but there are other ways you can help:

  • contact your local council - if you can offer temporary accommodation for less than 6 months
  • #StandWithUkraine (opens in a new page) - discover other ways of supporting people from Ukraine, including how to donate money and supplies
  • community sponsorship (opens in a new page) - get involved with community groups that support the resettlement of vulnerable people fleeing conflict
  • volunteering (opens in a new page) - find out how to volunteer in your local community and give your time to help others
  • Recognised Providers (opens in a new page) - support these organisations that help guests from Ukraine find sponsors in the UK

If you later decide you want to become a host, please come back to register for hosting opportunities.
