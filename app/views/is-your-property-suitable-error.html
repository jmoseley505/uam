Check if your property is suitable for hosting

Here’s a list of things for you to consider about the property you’re offering, before you commit to hosting people from Ukraine.

Ask yourself if your property has:

  • enough space to live comfortably
  • easy access to affordable public transport
  • shops and restaurants nearby
  • employment opportunities in the area
  • schools and education facilities nearby
  • opportunities to socialise in the area
