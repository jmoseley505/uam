Is your property suitable for hosting?

Here’s a list of things for you to consider about the property you’re offering, before you commit to hosting people from Ukraine:

  • enough space to live comfortably
  • affordable public transport connections
  • employment opportunities nearby
  • schools and education facilities nearby
  • opportunities to find or make friends nearby

Your property doesn’t need to meet every requirement.
