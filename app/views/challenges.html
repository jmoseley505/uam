There are some challenges

These are some of the challenges hosts may face, so you need to carefully consider:

  • covering additional household costs - you receive £350 a month, but isn't always enough
  • communicating with guests who don't speak English fluently
  • changes in family dynamics
  • differences in living habits, including household tasks and eating preferences
  • deciding on boundaries and how you want to live together.

Your property doesn’t need to meet every requirement.
