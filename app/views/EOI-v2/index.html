Have you considered

Local councils support Ukranians who are looking for homes, and rely on the genrous offers of people in their area orffering homes.

Register your availabilty so that you can:

  • be contacted when hosting opportunities come up in your area
  • stay up to date with training opportunites and guidance on becoming a host

Registration does not guarantee that your local council will contact you and find you a hosting opportunity.

Start now

Before you start make sure you have:

  • a clean, spacious and safe space for at least one adult
  • agreed with the rest of your household to offer a home for a minimum of 6 months
  • checked that you and your household don't hold a criminal record
