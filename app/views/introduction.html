We are prioritising finding new hosts for Ukrainians already in the UK

Ukranians who arrived in the UK over 6 months ago are looking for a new host. This is normally because circumstances have changed for their first host, such as deciding to moveor have a baby.

Guests already living in the UK typically need far less support from their hosts, as they may already have found work and settled into their local area.
